Topdown Shooter

An endless topdown shooter made in S&Box.

Procedural Maps

Each map is generated in a completely random way, ensuring that no run feels the same.

Multiplayer

Multiplayer functionality is a core feature, allowing multiple people to shoot enemies together!

Weapons

Weapon crates with randomized weapons and stats are placed around the map.

Different Environments

Each map has different sections that require you to use a variety of tools.

Enemies

Enemies can track you across the map.
